Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

Welcome to Uzair B2B Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent and secure experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information, our service terms, and your rights concerning our B2B website development and digital solutions.

1. Service Agreement & Payment Terms

Important: Our service costs are unique to each project's scope and complexity. A precise quotation can only be provided after a detailed consultation and understanding of your specific requirements.

  • Advance Payment: An advance payment of **50% to 30% (not below)** of the total project cost is mandatory to commence any work.
  • Project Preview: We will provide you with a sample or preview of your webpage/website for review and approval *before* the advance payment is due. This ensures you are satisfied with the initial direction.
  • Milestone Payments & Edits: Once the website development is completed to the agreed-upon specifications, we will present it to you for final review, edits, and changes as per your feedback.
  • Final Payment & Go-Live: The website will only be made live and the dashboard (in case of dynamic websites) will be provided upon receipt of the **full and final payment**.
  • Included Services: Our comprehensive website development services often include professional photo and video editing to enhance your site's visual appeal and content quality.
  • No Cancellation or Refund: Please note that once the advance payment has been submitted, **no cancellation or refund is allowed** under any circumstances. We commit resources and time upon receipt of your advance.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information to understand your project needs and to communicate effectively:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and address when you contact us via forms or email.
  • Project Details: Information related to your business requirements, project specifications, design preferences, and content shared for development purposes.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device and how you interact with our website (e.g., browser type, IP address, pages visited), collected via analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To understand your project needs and provide accurate quotations.
  • To communicate with you regarding your project's progress, updates, and feedback.
  • To deliver the services you have requested, including website development, design, and related digital solutions.
  • To improve our website, services, and client experience.
  • To send periodic emails regarding project updates or relevant information (you can opt-out at any time).

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal and project information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure communication channels and restricted access to data.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.

We may use third-party service providers to help us operate our business and the Site or administer activities on our behalf, such as sending out newsletters or surveys. We may share your information with these third parties for those limited purposes provided that you have given us your permission.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use "cookies" to enhance user experience. Your web browser places cookies on your hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them. You may choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you do so, note that some parts of the Site may not function properly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Request transfer of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we are relying on consent to process your personal data.

Uzair B2B Solutions has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage Users to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect. You acknowledge and agree that it is your responsibility to review this privacy policy periodically and become aware of modifications.
